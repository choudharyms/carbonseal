**Welcome, and thank you for your interest in contributing to Carbon Seal!**

Carbon Seal is more than just code; it's a commitment to a transparent, verified, and sustainable future for our planet's blue carbon ecosystems. By leveraging blockchain for immutable records and satellite data for verification, we are building the trust layer for the voluntary carbon market.

Whether you're fixing a bug, improving documentation, or building the next big feature, your contribution helps us certify and protect vital environmental assets like mangroves and seagrasses.
